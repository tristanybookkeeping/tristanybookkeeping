Frequently asked questions

Do you work remotely?
Yes. Everything runs through QuickBooks Online and email, so I work with businesses across Ontario without needing to be on-site. Documents are exchanged securely — never by sharing passwords.
Do I need QuickBooks Online?
It's the platform I work in, and I can set it up for you if you're not on it yet. If you're on spreadsheets or another system, setup and migration is part of what I do.
Can you clean up past bookkeeping?
Yes — catch-up and cleanup work is a core service, whether you're a few months or a few years behind.
Do you handle HST/GST returns?
Yes. I prepare HST/GST returns and file them with proper CRA authorization, with deadlines tracked so nothing is missed.
How quickly can we get started?
Usually within a few business days of our first conversation. Send a message with where your books stand and I'll reply within one business day.
What does it cost?
Most monthly clients pay a flat monthly fee based on transaction volume — no hourly surprises. I'll give you a fixed quote after a short conversation about your books.
